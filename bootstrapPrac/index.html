Welcome to Aggie Aux!

Aggie Aux is a place where Aggies can meet other Aggies and talk about music! Despite the tumultuous year we have experienced with COVID-19,it has brought people more closer together despite being long-distance in the form of music. Music has become a language in which we truly learn about how people are really like. Come create or join and room and listen to some tunes!
